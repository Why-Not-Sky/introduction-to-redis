Clustering and High Availability

Highlighting its role as an data-store in the enterprise, Redis offers two operational modes; clustering and high availability for increased use and reliability as core IT infrastructure.

Start by downloading Redis 3.0 and installing by doing the following: 

$ wget https://github.com/antirez/redis/archive/3.0.0-rc1.tar.gz
$ tar xvf redis-3.0.0-rc1.tar.gz 
$ cd redis-3.0.0-rc1
$ make
  

$ pip install redis-py-cluster

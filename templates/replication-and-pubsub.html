Replication and Pub/Sub

Redis supports two models of distributed data processing across different machines. Redis's master-slave replication is simple to use with horizontal scaling across multiple slaves connecting asynchronous with a master Redis instance. In addition, Redis is well suited to supporting the Publish/Subscribe model where publisher send messages to one or more channels that subscribes monitor and then react to the messaging queue.
